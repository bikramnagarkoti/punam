Skills

My technical level

Standards of accounting

Skills in standard principles of accounting, such as the GAAP, International Financial Reporting Standards or pro forma accounting, ensure a minimum level of consistency when preparing financial statements. These standards include a common set of principles and procedures for accountants to follow in preparing financial statements. This skill is important to maintain consistency in recording and reporting your organization’s finances as well as maintaining consistency for investors comparing your organization’s financial statements with those of other companies.

Knowledge of regulatory standards

Since accounting requires managing a company’s finances, it is important to have an understanding of regulatory standards surrounding corporate and public finances. Understanding applicable regulatory standards is important to ensure compliance in meeting financial reporting requirements and integrity in accounting.

Business knowledge

With my experience at chartered accountant firm as an intern as well as subsequent experience, I acquired the knowledge of various business ranging from trading to banking companies. It helps me to grow your business outside of finance and accurately assess, record and report your financial needs.

Software proficiency

I am proficient in work processing and spreadsheet applications as well as specialized accounting software; SAP and Tally.

Data analysis

Data analysis is a necessary skill for accounting. It involves the ability to derive meaningful information from raw data. This skill allows me to create informational financial reports based on financial data such as account budgets, revenue and expenses. It also allows me to verify the accuracy of financial data for data integrity and reporting consistency.

Attention to detail

It helps me to ensure consistency and accuracy in financial reporting. It allows me to verify financial data and generate accurate financial statements.
